MANUAL "CHECK BY PHONE"

Step 1. How to install the Klever wallet

To register in the project using your smartphone, you need to install the Klever application. This is your personal wallet for Tron cryptocurrency, which will store the earned funds. The wallet belongs only to you and is used for automatic registration and authorization on the site.

1) Download the application for your smartphone: Klever

2) We carry out the basic registration procedure. The login and password of the account should be securely stored in several places (preferably printed), as well as the 12 created words - this is the access key to the wallet. In the event of a LOSS of these control words (keys), access to the wallet will be impossible to restore.

Step 2. How to buy Tron cryptocurrency First, you need to find out the address of your Tron wallet, which you made in step 1.

1. Open the Klever app
2. Go to the Me tab, it is located at the bottom of the screen
3. Open the item "My wallet"
4. Copy your address from the blue card.

It is recommended to immediately buy at least 4 AUTOPOOL, which, together with registration, amounts to 8000 TPX.

Note: When depositing with Bank cards, the exchange may for the first time to verify the card. Usually it's a photo of the card in the background page exchange. Subsequent replenishments are made instantly. If You do not want to verify the exchange of your card, that you can first recharge QIWI or Яндекс.Деньги or Payeer.com with my credit card (through the interface either of these two payment systems, and not through the exchanger), and then have such payment system through the exchanger to change to an Ethereum wallet without the card verification.

1. Go to the website https://changelly.com
2.Choose the appropriate payment system with which you want to exchange for the required Tron currency
3.Choose the right service with the best rate
4. Go to the website of the selected exchanger and follow the simple instructions. The exchange is usually automatic and very fast
Make sure your Tron wallet has received funds

Step 3. How to register in the Tronmatrix project via Klever
1) You need to copy the link https://tronmatrix.github.io and paste it in your Klever browser

2) Follow the orange link "Registration in https://tronmatrix.github.io"
